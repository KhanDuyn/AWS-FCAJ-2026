### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
I find the working environment at FCAJ to be very friendly and professional. It creates a comfortable, approachable, and unconstrained atmosphere for interns. The workspace also facilitates concentration and enhances work efficiency.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
The admins always closely followed the internship progress and guided us on how to solve problems. They were always ready to fully support with documentation, procedures, and answer questions during the internship, helping us complete our work on schedule.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
The tasks performed during the internship are directly related to the Information Technology major, especially in the field of cloud computing and AWS infrastructure. Through practicing labs and deploying services on AWS, I had the opportunity to apply the knowledge I learned into practice, while also accessing and learning more about many new services and technologies for my study and professional development.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
The internship program helped me improve my self-learning skills, research technical documentation, and solve problems. Besides, I got to practice time management skills, work according to a plan, and use AWS services in real-world situations.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
The team members always have a high sense of self-discipline in their work. They are always ready to help each other complete tasks and achieve common goals.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
The company has a clear roadmap, providing documentation as well as company regulations for interns to learn and follow. FCAJ Community Day is organized weekly to help everyone exchange knowledge and enhance their understanding of current technology.


### Additional Questions
- What were you **most satisfied** with during your internship?  
I was most satisfied with being able to access many AWS services through practical lab exercises, while receiving dedicated guidance from the admins during the learning and practice process.  
- What should the program **improve** for future interns?  
More Community Day sessions could be added to share experiences and real-world projects so that interns can better understand how to apply knowledge to their work.  
- Would you **recommend this internship** to others? Why?  
Yes. Because the internship program has a clear roadmap, the content is suitable for beginners, the admins support enthusiastically, and it creates conditions for interns to learn and develop professional skills.  


### Suggestions & Expectations
- I hope the company will organize more Community Day sessions sharing real-world projects so that interns can better understand how to apply knowledge to their work.  
- I hope the company will consider organizing more outdoor activities to connect the members participating in the internship program.
