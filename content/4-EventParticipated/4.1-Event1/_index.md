# Summary Report: "FCAJ Community Day"

## Event Information

- **Event Name:** FCAJ Community Day
- **Date:** May 30, 2026
- **Time:** 09:00 AM – 12:00 PM
- **Location:** Bitexco Financial Tower, Ho Chi Minh City
- **Role:** Attendee

---

# Event Objectives

FCAJ Community Day was organized to create opportunities for students, developers, and technology enthusiasts to connect and learn from speakers with extensive experience in entrepreneurship, product development, cloud computing (AWS), soft skills, and psychology.

The event aimed to achieve the following goals:

- Share experience in building technology products from actual user needs.
- Introduce AWS technologies for system development and deployment.
- Guide effective methods for learning and practicing AWS.
- Raise awareness about self-confidence and the ability to overcome fear in study and work.
- Share experience in participating in Hackathons and developing technology projects.

---

# Session 1: Tu Vi Dai Viet – Transforming Traditional Knowledge into a Technology Product

The presentation by Nghia Tran introduced the journey of building the Tu Vi Dai Viet platform, a product that combines traditional horoscope knowledge with modern technology to deliver a more accessible experience for users.

### Key Topics

- Limitations of traditional horoscope services, such as high cost and difficulty for young people to access.
- Building a system based on data, business logic, and user experience.
- Combining Eastern Horoscope (Tu Vi Dau So) and Eight Characters (Bat Tu) to improve result accuracy.
- Migrating infrastructure to AWS to increase scalability and automate the deployment process.
- Utilizing Amazon Bedrock to support building AI features in the system.

### Key Takeaways

- A technology product can scale to serve millions of users.
- AWS helps the system run stably, flexibly, and scale easily.
- Combining data, AI, and user experience is a key factor in modern product development.

---

# Session 2: The Importance of Self-Confidence

Quynh Nhu Nguyen Thi shared the causes of lack of confidence and how to cultivate self-confidence in study and work.

### Key Topics

- The impact of lack of confidence on communication and career development.
- Psychological causes leading to lack of confidence.
- Methods to build self-confidence through daily action and practice.
- The role of communication and collaboration in personal development.
- The message of mastering fear instead of avoiding it.

### Key Takeaways

- Self-confidence is a skill that can be trained over time.
- Communication and collaboration help enhance confidence in study and work environments.
- Daring to take action is the first step to overcome personal limitations.

---

# Session 3: Enhancing AWS Skills with Cloud Quest and Floci

Thai Linh Huynh introduced the AWS learning roadmap through hands-on practice platforms and simulated Cloud environments.

### Key Topics

- Introduction to AWS Cloud Quest and hands-on learning roadmaps.
- Digital Badge system to validate AWS skills.
- Floci tool to simulate AWS services in local environments.
- Comparison of advantages and limitations between simulated environments and real AWS.
- Resource and cost management experience for AWS beginners.

### Key Takeaways

- Learning AWS effectively requires combining theory and practice.
- Floci helps reduce costs during application development and testing.
- Managing Cloud resources properly helps limit unexpected costs.

---

# Session 4: The Iceberg of Procrastination

Khac Uy Pham shared a psychological perspective on the true causes of procrastination and how to overcome it.

### Key Topics

- Analyzing the "iceberg" model of procrastination.
- Distinguishing between laziness and underlying fears.
- The impact of procrastination on project progress and work efficiency.
- Managing emotions instead of focusing solely on time management.
- Step-by-step methods to overcome fear and start taking action.

### Key Takeaways

- Most causes of procrastination come from psychological factors.
- Emotion management is an important factor in improving work efficiency.
- Small but continuous actions help build self-confidence and discipline.

---

# Session 5: Sharing from Hackathon Teams

The Hackathon teams introduced their product building processes, the challenges they faced, and their experiences deploying projects in a short time.

### Key Topics

- The SynthHunter project using AI for voice verification and fraud detection.
- The Vortex project optimizing recruitment processes using a digital platform.
- The product development workflow from idea to prototype.
- Lessons on teamwork, continuous testing, and improvement.
- Experience participating in Hackathon competitions.

### Key Takeaways

- Successful projects all start from solving real-world needs.
- Teamwork and proper task delegation help improve product development efficiency.
- Hackathon is a great environment to practice technical and soft skills.

---

# AWS Technologies Introduced

Throughout the event, speakers introduced various AWS services and tools, including:

- Amazon ECS
- Amazon RDS
- Amazon Bedrock
- GitHub Actions
- AWS Cloud Quest
- Floci

These technologies support building modern Cloud systems with scalability, automated deployment, cost optimization, and AI feature integration into products.

The session sharing AWS Cloud Quest and Floci also helped me better understand the AWS learning path, how to practice effectively, and how to control costs when using Cloud services.

The presentations from Hackathon teams also motivated me to keep learning, building products, and participating in more technology activities in the future.

In general, the event helped me expand my knowledge of AWS, AI, and product development, while providing motivation to continue training both professional and soft skills.
